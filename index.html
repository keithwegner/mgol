Rule: live cells survive with 2 or 3 neighbors; dead cells are born with exactly 3. Newborn species inherit local influence, with optional mutation and predation. Shortcuts: Space play, S step, R randomize, C clear, G grid, T trails, 1–4 tools.

Ecosystem Life rules and shortcuts

This variant keeps Conway’s original birth/survival logic. The twist is that every live cell belongs to a species. A newborn cell chooses its species from the three parents using the selected ecology mode. Predator Ring adds a rock-paper-scissors conversion pressure; Diversity Bias resists monocultures; Mutation Storm creates unstable, colorful invasions.
